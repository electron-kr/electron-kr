Electron Korea

Welcome! Electron Korea is users group for loving people who want to use and invest about technologies related to Electron developed by Github.

Meetup

We have annual meet-ups once a month in Korea to share our knowledge and experience in offline. Please come to us if you have an interesting of Electron. More information to visit out meetup
